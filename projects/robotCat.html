Robotic Pet Cat

2021

Project Idea

While having a pet in video gams can entertaining, the lack of physical interaction may feel less fulfilling. But taking care of a real pet requires time and efforts that might not be feasible for everyone. The robotic pet cat is an interacrive project designed to bridge the gap between these two experiences.

Project Implementation

Servos -- move the cat's tail and eyebrows.

Fan -- will turn on when the cat wants to blow the bubbles.

LED panel -- display the cat's emotions and needs.

Arduino -- positioned inside the cat and control physical components mentioned above.
