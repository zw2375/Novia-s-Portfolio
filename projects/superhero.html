Project Idea

Being as one of the mainstream cultures, superheroes has gained great popularity worldwide. However, their gender representation has not always been as progressive as their powers suggest.

This project aims at providing an insight of the gender representation of superheroes. It analyzes 733 superheroes who comes from Marvel, DC and some other movies and visualize the data from different perspectives: name, appearance, figure and power.

By combining the information data set for superheroes along with images getting from superhero API, this project is able to reach for a more detailed visualization.
