Vision

Year: 2021

Team: Zhinuo Wang, Xiaorong Tian

Project Idea

Vision is an interactive project designed to provide an immersive experience that explores and addresses the topic of appearance anxiety.

The character on the screen, wearing a hat, mirrors the user's movements as if it were a reflection of the user. Initially, the character appears cheerful. However, after the user takes a picture with the camera, a crowd suddenly appears behind the character, staring and whispering about them. The title, Vision, serves as a reminder that appearance anxiety is merely a distorted perception.

Project Implementation

Servos -- move the cat's tail and eyebrows.

Fan -- will turn on when the cat wants to blow the bubbles.

LED panel -- display the cat's emotions and needs.

Arduino -- positioned inside the cat and control physical components mentioned above.
